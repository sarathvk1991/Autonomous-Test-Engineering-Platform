This record is understood as exercising that Owner capacity under the "Configuration and Governance Authority" title it was commissioned with.

**Governance activities have completed.** Every stage `HB-001-R5-PROPOSAL` §4 names prior to Baseline — Proposal, Review, Disposition, Revision, Re-review, Approval, Approval Ratification, Condition Closure — is recorded, Frozen, and, where applicable, Closed (§7). None is reopened by this record.
