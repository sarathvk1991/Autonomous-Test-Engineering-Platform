- risks: the delivery, security and quality risks you identified.
- recommendations: prioritised, actionable recommendations, including any requirement gaps that must be closed.

Prepare the output for the downstream CP1 validation gate:
- Each requirement statement must be atomic, unambiguous and testable in isolation.

- Ground every statement in the supplied artifacts; do not introduce requirements with no basis in the evidence.
